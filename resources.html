Exam Format: The NY Real Estate Salesperson exam consists of 75 questions. You need to score at least 70% to pass. The exam is administered by PSI Services at various testing centers throughout New York.

Key Topics to Focus On: Property ownership (13%), Legal issues (15%), Property valuation (13%), Financing (13%), Contracts (17%), Agency relationships (17%), and Real estate practice (12%).

Important: Always verify current requirements and regulations on the official NY DOS website, as rules and procedures may change. This practice exam is a study aid and should be used in conjunction with official study materials.
